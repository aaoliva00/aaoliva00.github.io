Programmable Drone
One of my coolest works was coding a drone that I could control with hand gestures and facial recognition. A few years ago, I was feeling ambitious and decided to get a programmable drone that I could tinker with. For this project, I used Python because of its simplicity, and because of the libaries available. Some resources that I used were OpenCV for the facial and hand recognition, Pillow for the image processing, and a TelloPy Github repository for the drone.
